# Baked Beans

Recipe via Grandma Paparone.

## Ingredients

- 16-ounce can of beans
- 1/4 cup chopped onion
- 1/4 cup ketchup
- 2 tablespoons brown sugar
- 1/2 tsp worcestershire sauce
- 1/4 tsp dry mustard

## Instructions

1. **Preheat oven** to 350Â°F.
2. **Prepare:** Grease 1-qt casserole baking dish.
3. **Mix:** In a large bowl, combine ingredients.
4. **Fill:** Pour the mixture into the casserole dish.
5. **Bake:** 50 minutes or until bubbly.
