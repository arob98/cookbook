# Broccoli casserole

Grandma Paparone recipe.

## Ingredients

- 1 head broccoli, cook and drain 
- 1 can cream of mushroom soup
- 1 cup mayo
- 1 cup grated parmesan 
- 1 onion, chopped
- 2 eggs, beaten
- salt and pepper, to taste
- breadcrumbs and butter

## Instructions

1. **Preheat oven** to 350Â°F.
2. **Prepare the baking dish:** Butter casserole dish.
3. **Mix:** In a large bowl, mix chopped broccoli and all ingredients except breadcrumbs and butter. 
4. **Fill:** Pour the mixture in the casserole dish. Top with breadcrumbs and dot with butter.
5. **Bake:** 1 hour
