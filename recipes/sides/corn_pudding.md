# Corn pudding

Credit to Keith's Grandma Robertson.

## Ingredients

- 4 cups of frozen corn (or 6 ears of fresh corn)
- 1/2 cup butter 
- 1/4 cup sugar
- 2 eggs, well beaten
- 1/2 cup cream or evaporated milk
- 1 tbsp flour
- 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
- salt to taste
- dash of cinnamon

## Instructions

1. **Preheat oven** to 350Â°F.
2. **Prepare the pan:** Grease 2-qt casserole dish.
3. **Mix:** Heat butter until melted. Stir in sugar, flour, and remove from heat. Gradually stir in cream / milk. Add well-beaten eggs, baking powder, and salt. Mix well. Fold in corn, stir until well mixed.  
4. **Fill the pan:** Pour into casserole dish. Sprinkle with cinnamon.
5. **Bake:** Bake 45 min.
