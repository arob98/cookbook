# Beef tenderloin shish kabobs

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup beef broth
- 1/4 cut olive oil
- 2 tbps soy sauce
- 1 garlic clove, minced
- 1/4 tsp pepper
- 1 lb beef tenderloin
- 8 large mushrooms
- 2 small onions
- 1 bell pepper

## Instructions

1. **Prepare marinade:** Combine first 5 ingredients in a shallow dish. Add cut up beef.
2. **Marinade:** Cover and chill for 2 hours. Turn beef occasionally.
3. **Cook:** Put the beef and veggies on skewers. Grill for 5-7 min each side until done.
