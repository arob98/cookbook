# Caramel Corn

Recipe via Angie's Grandma Paparone.

## Ingredients

- 1 cup unpopped popcorn
- 1 cup dry, roasted peanuts
- 1 cup pecan halves or pieces
- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup brown sugar, firmly packed
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup light corn syrup
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp baking soda

## Instructions

1. **Preheat oven** to 250Â°F.
2. **Prepare:** Pop corn. Combine corn and nuts in a large roasting pan. 
3. **Mix:** Melt butter. Add sugars, corn syrup, and salt. Bring to boil. Boil for 5 min, stirring often. Remove from heat. Stir in baking soda. 
4. **Add:** Pour mixture over corn and nuts. Stir well. 
5. **Bake:** 45 minutes 

Cool and store.
