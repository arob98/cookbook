# Brownies

Love chocolate? You'll love these brownies.

## Ingredients

- 1 pkg (4 oz.) Unsweetened Chocolate
- 3/4 cup butter or margarine
- 2 cups sugar
- 3 eggs
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 1 cup flour
- 1 cup coarsely chopped pecans

## Instructions

1. **Preheat oven** to 350°F.
2. **Prepare the pan:** Line 13x9-inch pan with Reynolds Wrap® Aluminum Foil, with ends of foil extending over sides. Spray foil with cooking spray.
3. **Mix:** Microwave chocolate and butter in large microwaveable bowl on HIGH 2 min. or until butter is melted. Stir until chocolate is completely melted. Stir in sugar. Blend in eggs and vanilla. Add flour and nuts; mix well. 
4. **Fill the pan:** Pour into prepared pan.
5. **Bake:** Bake 30 to 35 min. or until toothpick inserted in center comes out with fudgy crumbs. (Do not overbake.) Cool completely. Use foil handles to remove brownies from pan before cutting to serve.
