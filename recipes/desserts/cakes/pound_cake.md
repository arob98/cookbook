# Million Dollar Pound Cake

Recipe from Angie's Grandma Paparone.

## Ingredients

- 1 lb butter, softened
- 6 eggs
- 3 cups sugar
- 4 cups all-purpose flour
- 3/4 cup milk
- 1 tsp almond extract
- 1 tsp vanilla

## Instructions

1. **Preheat oven** to 300Â°F.
2. **Prepare the pan:** Grease and flour loaf pan.
3. **Mix:** Cream butter. Gradually add sugar, beat at med speed. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Add flour, slowly. Mid-way, add milk, almond extract, and vanilla. 
4. **Fill:** Pour the mixture in the loaf pan. 
5. **Bake:** Bake 1 hour and 40 min or until a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.

## Notes

- Cool in pan for 15 min. 
- Remove from loaf pan and then cool completely on wire rack.
