# Pecan Pie

This recipe is courtesy from Angie's Grandma Paparone.

## Ingredients

- 1 refrigerated pie crust
- 1 stick of softened butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup light corn syrup
- 3 eggs, beaten
- 1 cup chopped pecans

## Instructions

1. **Preheat oven** to 350Â°F.
2. **Prepare the crust:** Place one pie crust in a 9-inch pie pan. 
3. **Mix the filling:** Slowly melt / warm butter. Add sugar, add syrup. Mix well. Add eggs and pecans. 
4. **Fill the pie:** Pour the mixture into the pie crust-lined pan. 
5. **Bake:** 1 hour
